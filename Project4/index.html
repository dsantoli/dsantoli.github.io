Captain America

The First Avenger

Hello, I'm Captain America, also known as Steve Rogers. I'm a fictional superhero appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. I was created by writer Joe Simon and artist Jack Kirby. I first appeared in "Captain America Comics" #1 in 1941. I am the first Avenger and the most loyal Avenger.

As Captain America, I am a symbol of freedom and justice, fighting against tyranny and oppression. With my iconic shield and unwavering determination, I've battled countless villains and stood up for what is right.
